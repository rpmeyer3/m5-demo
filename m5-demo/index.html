Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Sauté mushrooms in olive oil for 3 minutes.
  3. Wilt the spinach into the pan; season with salt and pepper.
  4. Whisk eggs with a splash of milk; pour over vegetables.
  5. Bake 12 to 15 minutes, until set in the center.
